History

Hazlow Electronics began in 1971 by filling a large demand for quality custom electronic timers, harnesses and cables required by companies such as Kodak and Xerox. 40 years later, Hazlow has maintained its harness and cable production of manufacturing. Its name is synonymous with hands-on leadership, featuring in-house engineering, manufacturing, materials and quality control. Hazlow continues to strive, with each customer, to provide a manufacturing relationship based on experience, practical know-how, and the desire to continue to improve.
